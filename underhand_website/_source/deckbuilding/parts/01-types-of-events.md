---
title: Types of events
---

At first, we need to realize that there are two types of events: _regular events_ and _event chains_. Regular events are usually recurring while event in a chain usually gets replaced by the next event from that chain.

We can also group events by the way they are added to the deck:

*   Summoning Events
*   Blessings
*   Random Events
*   Punishments

When the first deck is compiled, at first, summoning events are added dependently on which [gods](/gods/) you have already summoned. Then blessings you have selected are added into the deck. Lastly, some random events are randomly selected and added as well. Observation shows that about 8-10 random events are inserted.

Punishments appear as a reaction to your gameplay, some happen with a probability.
