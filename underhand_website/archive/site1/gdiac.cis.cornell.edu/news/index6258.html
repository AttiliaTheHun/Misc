Seven Games Accepted to Boston FIG

Our students regularly exhibit their projects at the Boston Festival of Indie Games. This year we had a spectacular string of success with seven (7) games accepted, our highest number so far. Many of these games will be familiar to those who attended our Spring showcase. The projects include:

  • Teddington (PC)
  • Deserted (PC)
  • Brought to Light (PC)
  • Division 12 (PC)
  • Aphelion (Android/iOS)
  • Squeak and Swipe (iOS)
  • Inari (iOS)

If you are in the Boston area the weekend of September 9th, come by the Festival of Indie Games to check them out.
