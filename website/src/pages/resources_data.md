Collection of freely available resources on the internet. All the services listed are free of charge, all the software listed is open source. This is not a public list, more like a personal list that I forgot to make private. I have not necessarily tested all the websites and software, I have read all the books though. There is no affiliation and no responsibility from our side regarding this page. Use it as you will.

Education
---------

*   [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) – Elementary and High school level education across different subjects. [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@khanacademy).
*   [PowerCert Animated Videos](https://www.youtube.com/@PowerCertAnimatedVideos) – Video series focused on basics of IT networking.
*   [Practical Networking](https://www.practicalnetworking.net/) – IT networking deep dive blog and [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@PracticalNetworking).
*   [Core Dumped](https://www.youtube.com/@CoreDumpped) – Computer and OS architecture focused channel.
*   [The Engineering Mindset](https://www.youtube.com/@EngineeringMindset) – Channel focused on electrical engineering.
*   [MalwareUnicorn](https://malwareunicorn.org/) – Reverse engineering and malware-related workshops. Hadn’t tested yet.
*   [Open Security Training](https://p.ost2.fyi/) – Free cybersecurity courses.
*   [3Blue1Brown](https://www.3blue1brown.com/) – Animated mathematics videos for high school and above. [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/3blue1brown).
*   [HardBreak](https://www.hardbreak.wiki/) – Hardware hacking oriented wiki.
*   [ChalkTalk](https://www.youtube.com/@chalktalkmath) – Cryptography, math, science YouTube channel.
*   [OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/) – Free courses and materials from MIT.
